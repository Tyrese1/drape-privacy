Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Delightful Apps (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information in connection with the Drapé mobile application (the “App”). Drapé is a creative tool that uses artificial intelligence to visualize how a garment design or fabric might look on a person, from photos you provide.

We built Drapé to be privacy-first: you don’t need an account to use it, and most of your data stays on your device. Please read this policy together with our Terms of Service.

Quick summary. No sign-in or account. Your profiles, saved looks and settings are stored locally on your device. To create a visualization, the photos you choose and a text prompt are sent to Google’s Gemini AI to generate your result. We don’t sell your personal data and we don’t use your photos to identify you or to build advertising profiles.

1. Information we process

Information you provide

  • Photos & images you add — a photo of a person, fabric swatches, and any design/style reference image — so the App can generate a visualization.
  • Profiles & measurements you choose to save (e.g. a name and body/garment measurements).
  • Design names and other text you optionally enter.

Information created in the App

  • Generated looks and the accompanying fit-analysis text, and your in-app preferences (measurement unit, theme, etc.).

Purchases & subscriptions

  • Purchase and subscription status. Payments are processed by your app store. We and our subscription-management provider receive your purchase/entitlement status and a pseudonymous app identifier — not your full payment card details.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical & diagnostic data such as app version, device model and operating-system version, and crash/performance diagnostics, used to keep the App working. Where this is provided through your app store’s standard developer tools, it is handled under their policies.

We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number or contacts, and the App does not require you to sign in.

2. How we use information

  • To provide the core feature: generating a garment/fabric visualization from your photos.
  • To save your profiles, looks and settings on your device so they persist between sessions.
  • To process and validate purchases and manage subscription entitlements.
  • To operate, maintain, debug and improve the App (e.g. fixing crashes).
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

3. AI processing of your photos

To create a visualization, the image(s) you provide and a text prompt are transmitted securely to Google, which processes them using its Gemini AI models to produce your result and return it to the App. This processing is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy. Images are sent only to fulfil the request you initiated.

  • We do not use your photos to perform facial recognition to identify individuals.
  • We do not use your photos to serve advertising or to build advertising profiles.
  • Google processes the images under its own terms and privacy policy. It may process them transiently to generate the output and in accordance with its own data-handling practices.

5. Storage & retention

Your profiles, saved looks and settings are stored locally on your device. You can remove them at any time by deleting individual looks or profiles, clearing your lookbook, or uninstalling the App — which deletes that data from your device. We do not keep our own cloud copy of your lookbook. Images sent for AI processing are handled by the AI provider for the duration needed to generate your result, per its policy.

6. Device permissions

  • Camera — to take a photo of a person, fabric or design (only when you choose to).
  • Photos / media — to let you pick an existing image (only when you choose to).
  • Internet — to send a request to the AI provider and process purchases.
  • Notifications — to tell you when a render is ready.
  • Vibration — to signal when processing finishes.

You can manage these permissions in your device settings.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information, including transmitting images to the AI provider over encrypted connections. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and to limit what is collected in the first place.

8. International data transfers

AI processing and our service providers may operate on servers located in countries other than your own. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards as required by applicable law.

9. Children’s privacy

Drapé is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. Do not upload a photo of a child without the consent of a parent or guardian. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. Your rights (EEA / UK and similar regions)

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, and to data portability. Because most data is stored only on your device, you can exercise many of these rights directly in the App (delete looks/profiles, clear your lookbook, uninstall). For other requests, contact us using the details below. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority. Where we process personal data, our legal bases include performing the service you request, your consent, and our legitimate interests in operating and improving the App.

11. Your California privacy rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to correct inaccurate information. We do not sell or “share” personal information as those terms are defined under California law, and we do not use sensitive personal information for purposes requiring an opt-out. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights. To make a request, contact us below.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. Your continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
